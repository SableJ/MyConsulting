About Me

Sable Jones, born in New Castle, Pennsylvania, is currently a freshman in the Games + Sim Major at Miami University, Oxford, Ohio where they are also aiming for a minor degree in Photography. Their main focus of work is design and art. This includes but is not limited to logo design, website layouts, 3D modeling, 2D art, and photography. Sable currently holds positions as an intern with the Miami University Esports program under Media Production, as well as acting as the DEI Representative for Hillcrest Honors Dorm Hall. Their dream work is to design character model sheets for an indie game company or animation studio.
